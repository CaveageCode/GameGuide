FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Q: WHAT IS SPRITELAND?

A: SPRITELAND IS A FREE-TO-PLAY, BROWSER-BASED MMORPG WHERE YOU CAN FREE ROAM, COMPLETE QUESTS, GATHER ITEMS, AND FIGHT MONSTERS IN A 2D FANTASY WORLD WITH A CRYPTOCURRENCY-BASED ECONOMY.

Q: HOW DO I EARN IN-GAME ITEMS?

A: ITEMS CAN BE OBTAINED THROUGH MONSTER DROPS (VARYING PROBABILITIES), COMPLETING QUESTS AND EVENTS, OR PURCHASING FROM IN-GAME SHOPS.

Q: ARE THERE GUILDS IN SPRITELAND?

A: YES, YOU CAN CREATE OR JOIN GUILDS WITH UP TO 50 MEMBERS TO SHARE ACHIEVEMENTS AND EXPERIENCE.

Q: WHAT ARE SKINS AND HOW ARE THEY OBTAINED?

A: SKINS ARE COSMETIC ITEMS THAT DO NOT AFFECT STATS OR LEVEL REQUIREMENTS. THEY CAN BE OBTAINED BY BURNING FOUNDERS NFTS OR PURCHASING THEM FROM THE IN-GAME SHOP.

Q: HOW DO I GET PETS IN SPRITELAND?

A: PETS CAN BE OBTAINED AS VERY LOW PROBABILITY DROPS FROM MONSTERS. ONCE OBTAINED, YOU CAN TRAIN THEM TO ASSIST YOU IN COMBAT.
